Our Story

Sixty-five years
of beautiful floors.

Family-owned. Expert-installed.
Locally trusted since 1972.

“We didn’t set out to build a flooring business. We set out to make people proud of where they live.” — Keith Frederickson Sr., Founder  ·  1972
1972

Est. 1972  —  Locally owned

Where it all began.

Keith Frederickson Sr. opened a single showroom on Main Street with three employees, two sample boards, and one conviction: that families deserve floors they’re proud to walk on.

Chapter 01  —  The Beginning

One showroom.
One family.

In the early days, every installation was done by Keith Sr. himself. He believed the people laying the floor should know the family living on it. That belief shaped everything that followed.

Chapter 02  —  The Expansion

Three showrooms.
Same handshake.

By the 1980s, three generations of the same neighborhoods had walked through our doors. We opened two more locations — but the promise stayed the same: measure it yourself, install it yourself, stand behind it yourself.

65+ Years in business

Chapter 03  —  Today

Family-run.
Future-ready.

The second and third generation now leads Keith’s — carrying forward the craft and service that built this company one floor at a time. New tools, new showrooms, same founding values.
